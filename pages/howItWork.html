How Does it Work

Data Collection

Our team travels to where the problems are- we gather data and
intelligence from major stakeholders in the field. The process is designed to
mobilize for change, shape strategies, and drive execution for our clients.

Analysis

This phase is dedicated to reducing the friction between raw data
gathered and the users. At DronaMaps, we think of technology as a tool
to solve human problems, depending on the
requirements assessed on the field, we deploy a range of cutting edge technical solutions.

Bringing data to life- Visualisation

Maps are visuals that make territories graspable. Our maps integrated
with applications built on top of the Olly platform allow users to
experience, analyse, and visualise spaces within their context
